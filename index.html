Hello, I'm Akash

PhD Student in Forestry

Hello! I’m Akash Anand, a Ph.D. student at the University of Wisconsin–Madison, where I work in the SILVIS Lab under the guidance of Prof. Volker Radeloff.

My research lies at the intersection of Remote Sensing, Artificial Intelligence, and Biodiversity Conservation, with a special focus on understanding habitat dynamics and biodiversity patterns using satellite imagery.
